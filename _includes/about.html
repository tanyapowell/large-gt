What is A Large G&T?


A Large G&T is the brainchild of Gillian Holder and Tanya Powell. Their mutual love of all things pop culture has led them to create their own podcast.

Join them as they serve up a cocktail of chat on music, TV, movies, everyday life, hair and beauty from a Black British and female perspective, with a dash of randomness and ranting.


About Gillian

Gillian works in television but has been a blogger for years. She writes about topics as diverse as politics to sport to music, the latter of which comes as no surprise as she is a big music lover.

She also writes poetry, loves nail polish and anything hot pink.

About Tanya

Tanya is a Software Engineer by day and blogger/baker/photographer/diva by night. She is working one day at a time to encourage more women to join her in the technology industry.

She also has an unhealthy obsession with crisps and reality TV.
